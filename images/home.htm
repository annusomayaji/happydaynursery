Welcome to our nurseries

Hi, I’m Natasha and I am the founder of Boys & Girls Nursery, which is now in its 10th year. We have a group of nurseries based across Hertfordshire, catering for children aged from 3 months to preschool.

As a parent myself, I know we all want the very best for our children and when we can’t be with them, we need to be absolutely certain they’re receiving care and attention from a team of highly qualified, caring professionals who enjoy their job.

Our family run nursery group has been born from hard work and a desire to make sure that only the best care is available for your children and I am extremely proud of the team that provide this at our nurseries.

Croxley Green

Bustling nursery, purpose-built and with excellent outdoor facilities plus the added benefit of being situated in the impressive Croxley Park.

Rickmansworth

Spacious purpose-built nursery with a fantastic garden and bright base rooms conveniently located on the edge of Rickmansworth town.

Stanmore

Light, bright and spacious rooms in the heart of Stanmore with excellent transport links to the surrounding areas and central London.

Watford

Small yet perfectly formed nursery, centrally located in Watford, minutes away from Clarendon Road and Watford Junction Station.
